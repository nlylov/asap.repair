Repair Asap LLC brings authentic Moroccan Tadelakt lime plaster installation to New York City. Tadelakt is a centuries-old technique originating from the hammams of Marrakech — a waterproof, naturally antibacterial lime plaster that's polished with river stones and sealed with olive oil soap. The result is a seamless, luminous surface with subtle depth that no paint or tile can replicate.

Our artisans handle every step: surface preparation, multiple plaster layers, burnishing with polishing stones, and final soap treatment. Tadelakt works beautifully in bathrooms, shower surrounds, kitchen backsplashes, accent walls, and fireplace surrounds. Available in a wide range of earth tones — from warm sand and terracotta to cool gray and off-white. Every project includes flat-rate pricing and a 1-year workmanship warranty.

The Process

How Tadelakt Is Applied

01

Consultation & Color

We visit your space, discuss colors and finishes, and provide a detailed flat-rate quote. We bring Tadelakt samples so you can see and feel the material firsthand.

02

Surface Prep & Application

We prepare the surface, apply multiple layers of lime plaster, and hand-burnish each layer with polishing stones for that signature smooth, luminous finish.

03

Polishing & Sealing

The final layer is compressed and polished, then sealed with traditional olive oil soap to create the waterproof surface. Your space is transformed into something extraordinary.
