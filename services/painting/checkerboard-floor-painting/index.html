Repair Asap LLC offers professional checkerboard floor painting across all five boroughs of New York City. The painted checkerboard floor is a timeless design element — seen in classic NYC brownstones, pre-war apartments, and modern lofts alike. Whether you want a crisp black-and-white diamond pattern for your entryway or a custom two-tone color scheme for your kitchen, our painters deliver precision geometry with lasting durability.

Every project includes full surface preparation (sanding, filling, priming), hand-measured and taped pattern layout, two coats of premium floor paint, and a protective polyurethane topcoat. We work on hardwood, plywood, concrete, and previously painted surfaces. Flat-rate pricing, same-day scheduling, and a 1-year workmanship warranty on every job.

The Process

How It Works

01

Measure & Plan

We visit your space, measure the room, discuss colors and tile sizes, and provide a detailed flat-rate quote. You approve the design before we start.

02

Prep, Tape & Paint

We sand, prime, and prepare the surface. Then we measure and tape a precise grid, paint the base color, and apply the contrasting squares by hand.

03

Seal & Protect

After the paint dries, we apply 2-3 coats of high-durability polyurethane topcoat to protect against scuffs, scratches, and daily wear. Ready to walk on in 48 hours.
