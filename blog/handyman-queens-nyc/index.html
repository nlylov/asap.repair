5 Things to Check Before Hiring a Handyman in Queens

  • Are they licensed and insured? In NYC, contractors should carry general liability insurance. Always ask for proof.
  • Can they provide a COI? Co-ops and condos often require a Certificate of Insurance. Not all handymen carry one.
  • Do they offer flat-rate pricing? Hourly pricing can balloon quickly. Flat rates give you certainty.
  • Check Google and Yelp reviews. Look for consistent patterns, especially how they handle problems.
  • Are they local? A Queens-based handyman knows the buildings and logistics. Travel time affects cost.
