Narrative

From punch list to full apartment renovation

The owner originally asked for a short repair list: restore damaged window sills, repair an outlet area, adjust a kitchen fixture/hose, and handle small bathroom items after long-term tenants moved out. After the first window-sill restoration, she expanded the job into a complete apartment refresh before the next tenants moved in.

  • Restored damaged window sills
  • Full interior repaint
  • Painted doors and baseboards
  • Bathroom refresh: vanity + shower-head update
  • Kitchen fixture / hose correction
  • Click-lock vinyl flooring in the hallway
