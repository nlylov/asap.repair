The project

From worn-out rental to ready-to-rent

The previous tenants left the unit dated and damaged — scuffed paint, a grimy bathroom, worn flooring, and rotted window sills.

Over five working days the crew took it back to a clean, neutral, move-in-ready apartment the landlord could list right away.

Scope of work

  • Full repaint — walls, ceilings, and trim
  • Hardwood floors sanded and refinished
  • Bathroom refresh — tub, fixtures, and re-caulk
  • New click-lock vinyl in the hallway
  • Window sills rebuilt and trim repaired
  • Outlets and wall patches repaired
